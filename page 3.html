What you can do to help on earth day and in everyday life.

1. Make sure to reuse,reduce, and recycle. Those 3 things can help decrease your footprint.

2. Save water. You can do this by taking shorter showers.You can also save water when washing your hands. Make sure to turn the water off when you are scrubbing your hands. Same applies with brushing your teeth as well. As you are brushing, make sure that the water is turned off untill you need it.

3. Educate. Try and educate yourself on how you can be more sustainable and reduce your footprint.
